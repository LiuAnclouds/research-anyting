# ACM SIGKDD (KDD)

## Overview
CCF-A conference (~18% acceptance). Premier applied data science venue. Strong industry presence. For GNN anomaly detection: good fit if the method is validated on real-world data at scale. Emphasizes practical impact and deployment feasibility. Lower acceptance rate than NeurIPS/ICML but different evaluation criteria (less theory, more practical impact).
