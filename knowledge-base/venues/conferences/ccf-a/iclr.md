# International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR)

## Overview
CCF-A conference (~30% acceptance). Open review process (reviews are public). Strong focus on representation learning. For GNN: graph representation learning, self-supervised graph learning. For VLA/VLM: VLA architectures (InstructVLA, UniVLA both at ICLR 2026). Slightly higher acceptance rate than NeurIPS/ICML but still very competitive.
