# Pattern Recognition (PR)

## Overview
CCF-B journal published by Elsevier. Impact factor ~7.5, one of the highest among CCF-B AI journals. Strong preference for methods validated through extensive experiments across multiple datasets and conditions. Publishes work on graph-based pattern recognition, anomaly detection, and representation learning.

## Submission Requirements
- Length: 12-15 pages
- Double-blind review: No (single-blind)
- Code availability: Expected
- Extensive experimental validation: Required

## Fit Assessment
- **Strong fit**: Comprehensive experimental studies, multi-dataset validation, methods with clear pattern recognition framing
- **Moderate fit**: Pure theory without experiments
- **Weak fit**: Incremental improvements without substantial validation

## Typical Acceptance Timeline
- First decision: 2-4 months
- Total: 4-8 months
