# IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems (TNNLS)

## Overview
CCF-B journal with the highest impact factor (~10.2) in its tier. IEEE flagship for neural networks and learning systems. Expects rigorous methodology, theoretical analysis, and comprehensive experiments. More selective than most CCF-B journals.

## Submission Requirements
- Length: 12-15 pages (IEEE format)
- Double-blind review: No (single-blind)
- Code availability: Expected
- Theoretical analysis: Strongly preferred

## Fit Assessment
- **Strong fit**: GNN methods with theoretical guarantees, learning systems with formal analysis
- **Moderate fit**: Strong empirical work without theory (may be desk-rejected)
- **Weak fit**: Incremental improvements, application-only papers

## Typical Acceptance Timeline
- First decision: 3-6 months
- Revision cycles: 1-3 rounds
- Total: 6-12 months
