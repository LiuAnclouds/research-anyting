# Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR)

## Overview
CCF-A journal (IF ~6.0). Open access. Strong emphasis on theoretical contributions. Empirical papers are published but must demonstrate fundamental methodological insights, not just strong performance. For GNN work: only appropriate if the contribution includes formal theoretical analysis (expressiveness, convergence, generalization bounds).

## Submission Requirements
- Length: Unlimited
- Double-blind: No
- Code: Not required (but encouraged)
- Theory: Required (formal proofs)
