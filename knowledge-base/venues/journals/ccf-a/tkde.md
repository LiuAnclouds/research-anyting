# IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering (TKDE)

## Overview
CCF-A journal (IF ~9.5). Premier venue for data mining and knowledge engineering. The 2023 and 2025 comprehensive surveys on graph anomaly detection were published here. Expects rigorous methodology, strong theoretical analysis, and evaluation on diverse real-world datasets. For GNN anomaly detection: requires formal problem definition, complexity analysis, and evaluation on 6+ datasets.
