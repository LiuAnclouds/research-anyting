# IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence (TPAMI)

## Overview
The highest-impact journal in AI/Computer Vision (IF ~20.8). CCF-A. Publishes landmark contributions that represent significant advances over the state of the art. Expects both theoretical depth and comprehensive experimental validation. For GNN work: requires novel architecture with formal analysis, evaluation on 8+ datasets across diverse domains, and clear evidence of paradigm-shift contribution.

## Submission Requirements
- Length: 14-18 pages (IEEE format)
- Double-blind: No (single-blind)
- Code: Required
- Theory: Required (formal proofs or rigorous analysis)
- Supplementary: Permitted
