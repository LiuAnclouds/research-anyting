# Artificial Intelligence (AI Journal)

## Overview
CCF-A journal (IF ~6.5). The oldest and most prestigious AI journal. Publishes fundamental contributions to AI theory and methods. Empirical work is accepted only when it demonstrates a fundamental insight about intelligence or learning. For GNN/VLA-VLM work: only suitable if the contribution reveals a fundamental principle about learning or reasoning.

## Submission Requirements
- Length: 14-18 pages
- Double-blind: No
- Code: Not required
- Theory: Required (formal contributions)
